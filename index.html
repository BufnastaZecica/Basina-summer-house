Sun

With 2715 hours of sunlight during the year it has the most sunny days in a whole region. That is why Hvar Island bares nickname Sunny Island.

Sea

This tirquoise bay boasts some of the clearest, cleanest water you’ll ever see. The calm sea is great for snorkeling and very safe for swimming, making it a very family-friendly location.

And nothing else

Basina bay is pretty little rustic cove on the island’s north coast that’s small in size yet packed with character. It’s surrounded by plenty of shady trees and rocky headland with just one small restaurant.

Beaches. Common in, water's lovely!

There is a great selection of beaches on Hvar island. No mather if you are looking for a nice and quiet family beach to spend a beautiful day with your family or maybe you would prefer all day beach party, crowded or not, you will find it here. One thing is certain: here you will find clearest, cleanest tirquoise water you have ever seen!

Restaurants. Local and international.

For those who like to try and enjoy local food and dishes, gastronomic offer is definitely something to talk about. Restaurants prepare fresh catch from Adriatic sea accompanied by locally-produced vegetables, all this washed with some of the world wide known local wines. The meat lovers will enjoy in Daltatinska Pasticada , beef cooked for couple of hours in red wine, dry prunes and carrots , and many, many more… Vegetarians and kinds will find delicious offer made especially for them.

Night clubs.Beach bars.

If you are looking for a party time than Hvar town is a place you are looking for. Here, Hula Hula and Carpe Diem Beach Bars are waiting for you day and night. Night club Veneranda is a perfect place to finish your night out, dancing with your last drink in your hands and waiting for a beautiful sun rise.
But if you are looking for a less crowded places, than Jelsa town offers nice bars and restaurants for you. Stari Grad and Vrboska are quiet and romantic places with excellent gastronomic offer.
